=== KEY FUNCTIONARIES / LEADERSHIP ===

Founder:
- Prof. Vishwanath D. Karad | Founder, Avantika University
  Role: Established Avantika University as India's first design-centered institution. Initiated formation of the first nine private engineering colleges in Maharashtra. Serves as Chief Patron of MAEER's MIT Group of Institutions.

Executive President:
- Sunil Karad | Executive President, Avantika University
  Role: Managing growth and long-term success of educational establishments within MAEER's MIT Group, Pune. Oversees development of institutions serving 50,000+ learners annually. Trustee and Treasurer of MAEER's MIT Group. Founded Edugild — India's global education technology accelerator. Established 10+ new-age educational institutions.

Chancellor:
- Prof. Sanjay Govind Dhande | Chancellor, Avantika University
  Role: Oversees the university's strategic direction. Former Director of IIT Kanpur. Member of Scientific Advisory Council to the Prime Minister of India. Former member of TRAI (Telecom Regulatory Authority of India). Expert in Computer Aided Design, Rapid Prototyping, and Reverse Engineering. Founded PDPM Indian Institute of Information Technology Design and Manufacturing, Jabalpur.

Vice Chancellor:
- Dr. Nitin Rane | Vice Chancellor, Avantika University
  Role: Leads university academic and administrative operations. Expertise in Process Design, Energy Efficiency, Environmental Pollution Control, Thermodynamics, and Equipment Design. Has played a key role in setting up many institutes and universities for MIT Group.

Pro Vice Chancellor:
- Prof. Surendra N. Rahamatkar | Pro Vice Chancellor & Dean (School of Engineering)
  Role: Oversees institution building, academic administration, strategic planning, global and national rankings, accreditations, research and development. Editor-in-Chief of International Journal on Computer Science Engineering Trends. Mentor of Change for Atal Tinkering Labs under NITI Aayog. Collaborates with Oracle, IBM, Infosys, TCS, Microsoft, and Google India.

Registrar:
- Dr. Manesh Patil | Registrar, Avantika University
  Role: Institutional leadership and academic administration. Over 22 years of experience in higher education. Expertise in chemical engineering and sustainable design. Recipient of Best Teacher of the Year award (2013).
