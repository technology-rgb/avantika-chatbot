=== ABOUT AVANTIKA UNIVERSITY ===

Avantika University is India's first design-centered university, established in 2017 through an Act of the Madhya Pradesh State Legislature. It is a state-private institution and part of the MIT Group, which operates 5 universities and 80+ institutions catering to over 70,000 students.

The university operates under the motto: "Education for the Next Generation to Build a Better World" — blending Art, Design, Innovation, Creativity, and Enterprise into an interdisciplinary curriculum with experiential learning.

Key Facts:
- Established: 2017
- Location: Vishwanathpuram, Lekoda Village, Ujjain – 456006, Madhya Pradesh, India
- Campus Size: 60 acres

Eligibility by Program:

B.Des:
- 10+2 pass from any stream (State Boards, CBSE, ICSE, ISC, CIE, IB, NIOS or equivalent)
- Minimum 50% aggregate marks
- Valid entrance exam: DST (Design Studies Test), UCEED, NID-DAT, or NIFT

M.Des:
- Bachelor's degree (12+3) from any stream
- Minimum 50% aggregate
- Valid entrance exam score required

B.Tech:
- 10+2 with Physics, English, Mathematics (compulsory)
- Minimum 50% aggregate in Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry/Vocational subjects
- English proficiency required
- Valid entrance exam: JEE / MPCET / CUET or state examination

BCA:
- 10+2 from any stream
- Minimum 50% aggregate marks
- English proficiency required

BBA:
- 10+2 or equivalent from any stream
- Minimum 50% aggregate marks from any recognized board

MBA:
- Graduation (Bachelor's degree) from any stream
- Minimum 50% aggregate marks
- Entrance exam as applicable (CAT/MAT/CMAT or university entrance)

B.Sc Agriculture:
- 10+2 or equivalent from any stream
- Minimum 50% aggregate marks

B.Sc Clinical Psychology:
- 10+2 or equivalent from any stream
- Minimum 50% aggregate marks

BA LLB / BBA LLB:
- 10+2 or equivalent
