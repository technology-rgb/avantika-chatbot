ABBREVIATIONS FOR AVANTIKA UNIVERSITY PROGRAMMES
FY = First Year, SY = Second Year, TY = Third Year.
BTech = Bachelor of Technology, BCA = Bachelor of Computer Applications.
BBA = Bachelor of Business Administration, BCom = Bachelor of Commerce.
BDes = Bachelor of Design, MDes = Master of Design.
MBA = Master of Business Administration, MCA = Master of Computer Applications.
IMBA = Integrated MBA, EMBA = Executive MBA.
BALLB = BA LLB (integrated law), BBALLB = BBA LLB (integrated law), LLM = Master of Laws.
BSc(A) = Bachelor of Science in Animation, BSc(CP) = Bachelor of Science in Computer Programs.
BCA(H) = BCA Honours, BTech(H) = BTech Honours.
SoD = School of Design, SoE = School of Engineering, SoM = School of Management, SoLPP = School of Law Policy and Planning.
